*** After installing ansible on VM try connect to worpress VM using:
	ansible wordpress -u vagrant --private-key vagrant_credentials -i hosts -m shell -a 'echo Hello, World'
	ansible -vvvv wordpress -u vagrant --private-key vagrant_credentials -i hosts -m shell -a 'echo Hello, World'

*** to send hosts and playbook.yml to VM (if not using script in vagrant file)
	sudo ansible-playbook -i /vagrant/provision/hosts /vagrant/provision/playbook.yml

*** Before writing the provisioning.yml playbook it's always good to test the playbook and VM connection with:

	---
    	- hosts: all
  	  tasks:
    	    - shell: 'echo hello > /vagrant/hello_world.txt'
